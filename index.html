Let's Connect

Born and raised in the islands, my foundation is built on aloha and doing things the right way. I have lived and traveled the word, from New Zealand to San Francisco, Thailand to Mexico.
These experiences have taught me versatility and adaptability in this ever—changing world. And my work as a web designer is no different. I write clean and elegant code with the end user in mind both on the front and back ends.

I started out as a self taught bread baker. I saw the need for local, freshly baked bread and took it upon myself to learn. Along with my girlfriend and amazing pastry chef, Sabeth Burad, we founded a microbakery, View Street Bread in Honolulu. After successfully operating the bakery for a year, we decided to move to San Francisco to pursue further learning. I worked as Bread Lead for Jane the Bakery in SF and specialize in sourdough breads, baguettes, and bakery operations.

We recently moved back to Honolulu, where Sabeth and I are in the process of opening a brick and mortar bakery. The self motivation and energy I put into baking is the same that goes into web design. I am passionate self starter and will bring all I have to your project. After learning the ins and outs of this world, I have dedicated myself to coding and designing websites that are lean, well organized, and easy to maintain. I specialzie in Squarespace websites and highly recommend them for anyone looking to create a beautiful, professional site that is easy to update.
